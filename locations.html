Cities in Sabaragamuwa

Sabaragamuwa province offers a delightful mix of cities for tourists to explore, each with its own unique charm:

Ratnapura: Known as the 'City of Gems', Ratnapura is a must-visit for its gem mines, bustling markets, and the magnificent Maha Saman Devalaya, a temple dedicated to the deity Saman.

Kegalle: This town offers a glimpse into rural Sri Lankan life. Visit the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, witness traditional handicraft making, and explore the scenic Belilena Caves.

Kuruwita: Adventure enthusiasts will love Kuruwita, a base for white-water rafting on the Kelani River and trekking in the surrounding rainforest.

Embilipitiya: Embark on a thrilling safari adventure from this gateway to Udawalawe National Park, renowned for its large elephant population.

Whether you're fascinated by gems, wildlife, or simply want to experience authentic Sri Lankan culture, Sabaragamuwa's cities have something special to offer.

Visiting Destinations in Sabaragamuwa

Beyond its charming cities, Sabaragamuwa boasts a wealth of other captivating destinations for tourists: Sinharaja Forest Reserve: Hike through this UNESCO World Heritage rainforest, teeming with endemic wildlife and stunning waterfalls. Adam's Peak: Climb this sacred mountain for breathtaking views and a unique pilgrimage experience. Bopath Ella: Admire this beautiful leaf-shaped waterfall and enjoy a refreshing dip. Ancient Temples: Explore Kuragala Buddhist Monastery and other historical temples scattered throughout the province. Udawalawe National Park: Spot elephants, leopards, and abundant birdlife on a safari adventure.
